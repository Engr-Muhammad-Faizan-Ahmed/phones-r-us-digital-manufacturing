Phones-R-Us

From Prototype to Smart Manufacturing — An Industry 4.0 Additive Manufacturing Journey

Project Overview

This project investigates additive manufacturing, digital traceability, FESTO automation, and Tecnomatix Plant Simulation within an Industry 4.0 manufacturing workflow.

FDM Manufacturing Journey

Over 50 print iterations were produced using Ultimaker S5, Ultimaker S3, and Bambu Lab P1S printers to optimise dimensional accuracy, snap-fit performance, and QR-code traceability.
