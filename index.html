Additive Manufacturing Journey
More than 50 print iterations were manufactured using Ultimaker S5, Ultimaker S3, and Bambu Lab P1S printers. Parameters including infill, print speed, support structures, and snap-fit tolerances were systematically optimised.

FESTO CP Lab Validation
Final parts were validated through the FESTO CP Lab automated assembly system. Measuring and Muscle Press stations became the critical validation stages for dimensional accuracy and snap-fit functionality.

Tecnomatix Plant Simulation
Tecnomatix Plant Simulation was used to analyse throughput, bottlenecks, worker allocation, printer utilisation, and annual production capability. Experiment Manager studies demonstrated that 3D printing capacity — not labour — was the primary system bottleneck.
