Project Overview

Over 50 design iterations were developed using Ultimaker S5, Ultimaker S3 and Bambu Lab P1S to manufacture precision phone casings validated through FESTO CP Lab automation.

The Engineering Journey

Daily PrintCity experimentation, parameter optimisation, snap-fit redesign, and final recovery after losing all printed parts two days before submission.

FESTO Validation

Critical validation through Measuring and Muscle Press stations ensured precise tolerance and successful assembly.

Tecnomatix Simulation

Simulation proved that printing throughput was the true bottleneck rather than labour, achieving 52,160 annual units.
