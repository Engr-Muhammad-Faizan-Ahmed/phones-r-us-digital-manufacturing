Project Overview
This project explores end-to-end digital manufacturing of 3D printed phone casings using FDM technology, integrated with Industry 4.0 systems including simulation, automation, and digital traceability.

Additive Manufacturing Journey
Over 50 iterations were produced using Ultimaker and Bambu Lab printers. Parameters such as infill, speed, and snap-fit tolerances were systematically optimised.
